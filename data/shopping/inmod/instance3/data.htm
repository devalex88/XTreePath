Set up a stylish office with ease with the Z Deluxe Desk Set. This modern set is perfect for the office or home office, and offers everything you need to get to work and stay organized.

The Z Deluxe Desk Set includes the Z Deluxe Large Desk, Z Deluxe Corner, and your choice of a small or medium desk with shelf. It is available in your choice of finish, and features a heavy duty powder coated steel frame with tempered glass desk tops and shelves. This set also has an optional Hang File & Pencil Tray, and Keyboard Tray. Its exceptional quality makes the Z Deluxe Desk Set ideal for commercial use.
