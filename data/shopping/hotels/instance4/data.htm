Overview

Occupying a Beaux Arts building dating to 1927, Park Central New York Hotel is within a block of famed concert venue Carnegie Hall and within a 5-minute walk of Manhattan’s world-renowned Broadway theater district. Prefer the great outdoors to the Great White Way? Central Park is just 3 blocks from the hotel. There, you can rent a rowboat at the lake, play a game of tennis, or visit the Central Park Zoo. The international boutiques and flagship department stores of Fifth Avenue start within a 10-minute walk of the hotel. For travel to sights farther afield, there are 7 subway lines located within 3 blocks of the Park Central.

Before heading out to a concert or Broadway show in the evening, visit the hotel's bistro, cityhouse, where the weeknight prix-fixe menu include choices such as lobster bisque and filet mignon. Watch the Seventh Avenue foot traffic through oversized windows as you indulge in a chocolate lava cake. After the show, enjoy a cocktail in the moody lobby lounge, Bar Bella.

Shed the stress of the day in the 24-hour fitness center, then retreat to your guestroom and sink into a bed with a pillowtop mattress and down comforter and pillows. Need to check email or finish up some work? You’ll find a desk with an ergonomic chair and wireless and wired high-speed Internet access (surcharge). Unwind with a video game (surcharge) on the flat-panel HDTV.

Recreation

At the 24-hour fitness center, you’ll find free weights, weight machines, and cardio equipment with individual TV screens.

Three blocks north of the hotel, 843-acre Central Park offers walking and running routes, bicycle rentals, tennis courts, and a 20-acre lake with rowboat rentals.

A short guide to Broadway - Times Square

Let the West Coast have Hollywood; Broadway’s lights are just as bright, and many screen actors come to the Great White Way to gain acting credibility. Area attractions include Carnegie Hall, Rockefeller Center, Radio City Music Hall, and the Museum of Modern Art. Resplendent St. Patrick’s Cathedral takes up a block at Fifth and 50th. Once seedy, Times Square now hosts ABC, MTV, and NASDAQ.

Recommended Trip type: Romance "Highly recommend the Park Central Hotel"

Reviewed by Mike C of Round Lake, IL on Jul 21, 2011

Great location! 2 blocks from the park, 8 blocks to Times Square, practically across the street from Carnegie Hall (and deli). There is a subway station right on the corner (N,Q,and R lines) which you can take downtown, or transfer to almost any other line at Times Square. My wife and I were very pleased with the hotel, itself. Our room was very clean, despite what some reviews had said. We did not sit in the bar or eat in the restaurant, so I cannot comment on either.
