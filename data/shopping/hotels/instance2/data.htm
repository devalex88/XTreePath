Overview

Location.
This Chicago hotel is in the River North district, just north of the downtown business core. The Magnificent Mile is just two blocks east. Millennium Park is one mile away. A CTA (Chicago Transit Authority) station, about one block from the hotel, provides easy access to destinations around town.

Hotel Features.
Comfort Inn & Suites Downtown serves a complimentary Continental breakfast, including hot waffles, every morning. The hotel's fitness center includes a sauna and spa tub. A business center, activities desk, gift shop, and convenient guest-laundry facilities are also on site. A safe-deposit box is available at the 24-hour front desk. Self-parking is available for a discount.

Guestrooms.
This Chicago hotel offers 130 guestrooms and suites. All provide complimentary wireless and wired high-speed Internet access, as well as free local calls. Cable TV comes with premium channels. Other amenities include coffee/tea makers, desks, multiline phones, iPod docks, and complimentary weekday newspapers. In-room safes can accommodate laptops. Bathrooms include hair dryers, telephones, and Gilchrist & Soames luxury toiletries.

A short guide to Magnificent Mile - River North

Magnificent Mile, a part of Michigan Avenue from the Chicago River to Oak Street, appeals to tourists because of its flashy department stores and fancy restaurants. Several of the Windy City’s architectural wonders are here, including the John Hancock Center and the Old Chicago Water Tower. Just north of the Chicago River, River North is home to the city’s shopping, design, and gallery districts.
