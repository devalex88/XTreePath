Overview

Location.
This business-friendly Radisson is 1 block from Allstate Arena, 2 miles from O'Hare International Airport, and 2.5 miles from the Donald E. Stephens Convention Center. Downtown Chicago attractions are 12 to 15 miles away.

Hotel Features.
Complimentary airport-shuttle service is available 24 hours a day. The hotel's business center offers FedEx supplies, fax/copy services, and two computers. Sectioned-off work spaces in the lobby include desks, office supplies, and complimentary wireless Internet access. An outdoor pool is open seasonally, and a fitness center is open 24 hours a day. The Radisson Chicago O'Hare hosts a complimentary social hour Monday – Thursday evenings. The hotel also features a steakhouse, a breakfast café, and a lobby lounge.

Guestrooms.
Soundproofed, smoke-free guestrooms have private balconies and either adjustable-firmness or pillowtop mattresses. Wireless Internet access is complimentary. Desks come with ergonomic chairs. Cable TV includes premium HD channels. Other amenities include coffee/tea makers, and direct-dial phones with free local calls.

A short guide to Chicago O'Hare International Airport (ORD)

O'Hare is one of the world’s busiest airports. Its neighbor, Rosemont, is also a hub of activity. Big-name entertainers and sporting events headline at the Allstate Arena, while conferences and trade shows convene at the Donald E. Stephens Convention Center. Downtown Chicago is just a train ride away on the CTA Blue Line (the L), which runs 24 hours a day from the airport to the Loop.
