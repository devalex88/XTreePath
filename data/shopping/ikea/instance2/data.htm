-Adjustable shelves; adjust according to need.
Designer thoughts
"What was most fun about designing HEMNES workspace furniture was introducing modern functions in furniture with a more tradional look. Here you can find smart solutions for laptops, chargers and cables - all in a robust wooden desk and secretary. I also wanted to take the opportunity to make a cozy corner workspace, because a corner is a good place for concentrating on work. I hope you feel as inspired using the furniture as I felt designing it."
Designer Carina Bengs
