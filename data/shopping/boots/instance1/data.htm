Detailed product information

Boots Essentials Ceramic Hair Straighteners have all the features you need to leave your hair straight and beautifully shiny.

Floating ceramic plates ensure even heat distribution and glide through the hair leaving it incredibly straight and shiny.

Boots Essentials Hair Straighteners Technology:
  • Ceramic floating plates
 
Boots Essentials Hair Straighteners Temperature:
  • High heat of 200°C 
 
Boots Essentials Hair Straightener Features:
  • Salon length swivel cord for a tangle-free cable
 
Box Includes:
  • Boots Essentials Hair Straighteners
